Course Overview

This comprehensive Beginner Driving Course is designed for new drivers with little to no prior experience. It covers all fundamental aspects of driving, ensuring students develop a strong foundation in vehicle control, traffic laws, and safe driving practices. Our certified instructors provide personalized guidance, helping students build confidence behind the wheel.

What You'll Learn:

  • Vehicle controls and basic maneuvers (steering, braking, accelerating)
  • Understanding traffic signs, signals, and road markings
  • Rules of the road, including right-of-way and lane discipline
  • Parking techniques (parallel, perpendicular, angle parking)
  • Defensive driving fundamentals and hazard perception
  • Pre-trip inspections and basic vehicle maintenance
  • City driving and introduction to highway driving

Prerequisites:

Must be at least 16 years old with a valid learner's permit. No prior driving experience required.

Course Curriculum

Module 1: Vehicle Controls and Basics

This module introduces new drivers to the fundamental controls of a vehicle and basic operational procedures.

  • Steering Wheel and its operation
  • Accelerator, Brake, and Clutch usage
  • Understanding Dashboard Warning Lights
  • Mirror Adjustments and Seat Positioning
  • Basic Starting and Stopping Procedures

Duration: 2 Hours

Module 2: Traffic Rules and Road Signs

Learn about the essential traffic rules and road signs necessary for safe and legal driving on public roads.

  • Understanding Regulatory Signs (Stop, Yield, Speed Limit)
  • Navigating Warning Signs (Curves, Intersections, Pedestrian Crossings)
  • Rules for Turning, Lane Changes, and Roundabouts
  • Right-of-Way Principles
  • Importance of Traffic Lights and Signals

Duration: 2.5 Hours

Module 3: Parking Techniques

Master various parking methods crucial for urban and suburban driving environments.

  • Parallel Parking (Step-by-step guide)
  • Perpendicular Parking (Forward and Reverse)
  • Angle Parking
  • Parking on Hills (Uphill/Downhill with Curb)
  • Parking Safety Tips

Duration: 3 Hours
